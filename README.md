# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data our Discord bot ("the Bot") collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use the Bot in a Discord server, we may collect and store:

- **Discord User ID** — to identify you for leveling, moderation, and giveaway entries.
- **Discord Server (Guild) ID** — to store server-specific settings (welcome/goodbye messages, leveling config, etc.).
- **Message activity metadata** — such as message counts or timestamps, used to calculate XP/levels. We do not store the content of your messages.
- **Moderation records** — such as warnings, mutes, or bans issued through the Bot, including the reason and moderator who issued them.
